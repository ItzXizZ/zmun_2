About Us

BCMUN is an established Model United Nations conference based in British Columbia, Canada. Our mission is to provide both high-quality online and in-person Model United Nations Conferences for free! As we announce BCMUN2023 we are so excited to meet new delegates and reunite with those who have attended our past conferences. If you have any questions please feel free to contact us, either through email, or through our instagram linked below. We hope that our conference can inspire a new generation of politically-passionate individuals and look forward to seeing you all soon!

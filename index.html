British Columbia Model United Nations

The Third Iteration: November 11th - 12th
Fairmont Pacific Rim

Dear prospective Delegates and faculty advisors,

My name is Michael Xie and it is with my utmost pleasure to welcome you to British Columbia Model United Nations (BCMUN), taking place on November 11th and 12th, 2023. This year's conference promises to be an exciting and rewarding experience for all participants, as we engage in lively debates, negotiate and draft resolutions, and tackle some of the world's most pressing issues. For the past two years, BCMUN has been hosting its annual iteration online - enabling delegates from all around the globe to participate. This upcoming third iteration of BCMUN marks its first time being held in person. We are excited to host this year's conference at the Fairmont Pacific Rim, a prestigious 5-star hotel rated the number one hotel in Vancouver.

The Fairmont Pacific Rim is ideally located in the heart of Vancouver, offering an exquisite hotel that boasts elegance and sophistication. It is no surprise that it is the number one hotel in Vancouver, as its beauty is unparalleled. As you enter the hotel, you are greeted by a grand lobby that exudes luxury and style, with high ceilings, marble floors, and stunning artwork adorning the walls. The hotel's guest rooms are equally impressive, with plush bedding, sleek furnishings, and floor-to-ceiling windows that offer breathtaking views of the city skyline or the harbor. The bathrooms are spa-like, with deep soaking tubs and rainfall showers that provide a truly indulgent experience. Restaurants within the venue are also noteworthy, offering delectable cuisine and impeccable service in a variety of stunning settings. Whether you are enjoying a meal at the Botanist restaurant, having a meal at The Lobby Lounge, or taking in the views at The Roof, you will be surrounded by beauty and elegance. We are confident that this stunning setting will provide a conducive atmosphere for fruitful discussions and deliberations.

The Secretariat team has been working tirelessly to prepare for this conference, and we are proud of the outstanding program that we have put together. Our team has carefully selected and prepared a wide range of thought-provoking topics, each of which will challenge delegates to think critically and creatively, as they work together to find solutions to complex problems.

Together with the Secretariat and Staff team, I am committed to creating an unforgettable experience for all participants that will have a lasting impact beyond the committee room. I truly hope that BCMUN will help you develop a stronger voice, cultivate an interest in global affairs, and most importantly, form life-long friendships. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me through my email sg@bcmun.org. I am excited to meet everyone at BCMUN 2023!

Sincerely,

Michael Xie
Secretary General, BCMUN Conference 2023

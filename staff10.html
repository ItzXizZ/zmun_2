About Dean John



Dean, the Assistant Director at UNODC, is excited to see all of the bright and talented delegates debating at BCMUN. He is a Vietnamese high school graduate who is highly passionate about MUN and has always dreamed of participating in international events. Kickstarted in 2019, his MUN pathway has been filled with hurdles and obstacles. Starting from his beginning MUN, he didn't know how to talk or knew what a MUN was; however, after trying it for the second time, he became addicted. With his MUN journey spanning 15-20 as a delegate and chair, armed with a plethora of information from fellow organizers and Secretariat members, he is determined to make your MUN journey a success, turning you into a diplomat and an avid debater. Outside of the classroom, Dean loves hanging out with his friends, drinking boba, sightseeing, and all around being a teenager. Dean is ecstatic to see this year's iteration of BCMUN and make UNODC the best committee in the conference, no questions asked!

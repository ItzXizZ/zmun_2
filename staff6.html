About Smriti Tiwari



Smriti Tiwari, a 15 year old Sophomore from Seattle, is ecstatic to serve as your Chair for the UNHCR! Smriti first entered the world of MUN as a freshman, and has since then grown to love the exciting world of diplomacy and debate. Outside of Model UN, Smriti enjoys thrifting, cooking, and creating playlists for every situation possible. She also enjoys serving her school through ASB and Class Council. Smriti is greatly looking forward to meeting all of you in our wonderful committee, UNHCR, at BCMUN 2022!

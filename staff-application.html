  • Director

    The director is in charge of running the entire committee as well as reporting to the secretariat the progress of the committee. The director selects the topic, prepares the crises and writes the background guides. During the conference, the director is in charge of making sure there is a good flow of debate and that the committee is successful. A director applicant should have a lot of previous MUN experience and must be prepared to take on a big work load as they have to write background guides as well as manage the rest of the Dais team.

  • Assistant Director

    They are responsible for aiding committee Directors in upholding proper Parliamentary Procedure, managing committee logistics, observing debate, and answering delegate questions.

  • Chair

    The chair is in charge of maintaining order and running the proper MUN procedure during committee sessions especially during times where the director is busy reading over position papers or editing working papers. A chair applicant should be confident that they know MUN procedure and can maintain the order of a committee.
